🎯 Phase — Polar Plot

Polar plot showing phase angle and magnitude of oscillations per electrode. Distance from center = power. Angle = instantaneous phase. Trails show recent trajectory. When electrodes cluster together, the brain regions are synchronizing.

❤️ PPG / Heart Rate

Photoplethysmography measures blood volume pulse via infrared light. The BPM readout is smoothed. The waveform shows the cardiac cycle. Heart rate variability (HRV) can be observed in the spacing between peaks.
